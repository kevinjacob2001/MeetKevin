On the off chance that there wasn't early lockdown, at that point this place would have been a burial ground, cause there are many towns where appropriate clinical offices haven't come to till date, Early lockdown has given the country a high ground for the battle against COVID 19. Let's appeal to God for their health, If we can give them mental support then it would be incredible relief for them amidst the pandemic. Let's pray to God for them, their dear ones, for their great health, Let's abide by the laws being forced during this time and the guidelines by the WHO!
